Privacy Policy 隐私政策

Effective Date 生效日期: April 16, 2025

This privacy policy explains how the Cosplay Planner app (“we”, “our”, or “us”) handles your information. 本隐私政策说明了 Cosplay 计划鸭应用程序（“我们”）如何处理您的信息。

1. Information Collection 信息收集

We do not collect any personal data from users. All data you enter (such as character notes, tasks, and images) is stored only on your device. 我们不会收集用户的任何个人数据。您输入的所有信息（如角色笔记、任务、图片等）仅存储在您的设备上。

2. Data Usage 数据使用

The app does not send any data to external servers. All information is used solely for local functionality to help you organize your cosplay planning. 本应用不会向外部服务器发送任何数据。所有信息仅用于本地功能，帮助您更好地规划和管理 Cosplay。

3. Third-Party Services 第三方服务

Cosplay Planner does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking tools. Cosplay 计划鸭不使用任何第三方分析、广告或追踪工具。

4. In-App Purchases 应用内购买

If you choose to support the app via in-app purchases (e.g., buying a virtual milk tea), your transaction is handled by Apple. We do not receive or store your payment information. 如果您通过应用内购买（如“请我喝奶茶”）支持我们，交易将由 Apple 处理，我们不会接收或存储您的支付信息。

5. Data Deletion 数据删除

To delete your data, simply uninstall the app. All data stored on your device will be permanently removed. 如需删除数据，只需卸载本应用。保存在您设备上的所有数据将被永久删除。
